Effective Date: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lucky Bro Content Tool ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your data when you use our services to interact with TikTok.

1. Data We Collect

When you authorize our App via TikTok OAuth, we may access:
-Your TikTok Open ID and basic profile information (e.g., display name).
-Access tokens required to perform actions you explicitly trigger (video uploading).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the requested permissions (video.upload and video.publish) solely to publish educational or creative content that you have prepared within the App to your TikTok account.

3. Data Storage and Security

We do not store your videos on our servers. Access tokens are stored securely (locally or encrypted) and are used only for communication with the official TikTok API. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your authorization data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can revoke our App's access at any time through your TikTok account settings (Settings and Privacy > Security > Manage app permissions) or by contacting us at [YOUR_EMAIL].
